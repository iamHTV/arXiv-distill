# [2512.03373] LLM-Generated Ads: Từ Ngang bằng Cá nhân hóa đến Vượt trội Thuyết phục

## Vấn đề
LLMs ngày càng capable (khả năng) generating persuasive content (tạo nội dung thuyết phục). Nhưng effectiveness (hiệu quả) across different advertising strategies (chiến lược quảng cáo) chưa rõ. Cụ thể: LLMs có thể rival (cạnh tranh) human experts trong ad creation không? Và: personalization (cá nhân hóa) vs universal persuasion (thuyết phục phổ quát) — LLMs giỏi hơn ở đâu?

## Đóng góp
1. **Study 1 (n=400)**: Personality-based personalization — LLM ads đạt statistical parity (ngang bằng thống kê) với human ads: 51.1% vs 48.9% (p>0.05). Không có significant difference cho matched personalities
2. **Study 2 (n=800)**: Universal persuasion — LLM ads OUTPERFORM human ads: 59.1% preference rate vs 40.9% (p<0.001). Strongest: authority (63.0%) và consensus (62.5%) appeals
3. **Robust advantage**: Even với 21.2pp detection penalty khi participants nhận ra AI origin, AI ads vẫn outperform. 29.4% chose AI content despite knowing origin

## Phương pháp (tóm tắt cốt lõi)
Study 1: n=400 participants, personality-based ads tailored to openness và neuroticism. LLM-generated vs human-written ads. Metrics: Product Rating, Purchase Intention. Study 2: n=800 participants, multimodal ads based on 4 persuasion principles: authority, consensus, cognition, scarcity. Forced-choice preference metric. Qualitative analysis of AI advantage sources.

## Kết quả chính
- Study 1: LLM parity với human — 51.1% vs 48.9% (p>0.05)
- Study 2: LLM superiority — 59.1% vs 40.9% (p<0.001)
- Authority appeal: 63.0% AI preference
- Consensus appeal: 62.5% AI preference
- Scarcity appeal: no advantage — AI cannot overcome skepticism toward high-pressure tactics
- Detection penalty: -21.2pp khi recognized as AI — still outperforms
- 29.4% chose AI despite knowing origin
- AI advantage: sophisticated aspirational messages, superior visual-narrative coherence
- Near-zero marginal cost vs human experts

## Insight có thể áp dụng ngay
1. **LLMs cho ad creation — cost-effective**: LLM-generated ads rival/surpass human experts với near-zero marginal cost. Ví dụ: small business không budget cho copywriter → dùng LLM generate ads, especially authority và consensus appeals.

2. **Authority và Consensus appeals work best**: LLMs excel at synthesizing credibility markers và social proof. Ví dụ: khi generate product ads, emphasize (nhấn mạnh) expert endorsements (authority) và customer testimonials (consensus) — LLMs tạo content này better than humans.

3. **Scarcity appeals don't work**: AI cannot overcome consumer skepticism toward high-pressure tactics. Tránh scarcity-based ads từ LLMs. Ví dụ: "Limited time offer!" từ AI → consumers skeptical. Thay vào đó: "Trusted by 10,000+ customers" (consensus) → more effective.

## Giả định & Hạn chế
**Điều kiện để study work:**
- English-language ads only
- Western participant pool
- Specific personality traits tested (openness, neuroticism)

**Hạn chế paper thừa nhận:**
- Study 1 vs Study 2: different metrics — không direct comparison
- Personality matching only 2 traits
- Detection penalty study limited

**Hạn chế paper KHÔNG nói:**
- Cultural bias — Western participants only
- Product category effects — different products có thể different
- Long-term brand effects — short-term preference ≠ long-term brand building
- Ethical concerns — AI-generated deceptive advertising
- 58.4% detection accuracy — moderate, not high
