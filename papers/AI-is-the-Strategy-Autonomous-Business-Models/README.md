# [2506.17339] AI là Chiến lược: Từ Agentic AI đến Mô hình Kinh doanh Tự động

## Vấn đề
Most firms vẫn operate trong human-driven hoặc AI-augmented models. Nhưng chúng ta đang entering phase nơi agentic AI (hệ thống capable initiating, coordinating, adapting actions autonomously) có thể increasingly execute core mechanisms (cơ chế cốt lõi) của value creation, delivery, capture. Prior frameworks treat AI as tool support strategy — nhưng shift này reframes AI as THE strategy itself.

## Đóng góp
1. **Autonomous Business Models (ABMs)**: Distinct managerial và strategic logic trong age of agentic AI. AI executes core value creation, delivery, capture — không chỉ support
2. **Synthetic competition**: Strategic interactions occur at machine-level speed và scale. New form of competition giữa AI-led firms
3. **Cases**: Israeli startup (autonomy by design) + hypothetical Ryanair reconfiguration (AI-driven incumbent)

## Phương pháp (tóm tắt cốt lõi)
Conceptual framework: ABMs as distinct logic. Evolution: augmented → autonomous business models. Show how ABMs reshape competitive advantage qua agentic execution, continuous adaptation, gradual offloading of human decision-making. Illustrative cases: Israeli startup pursuing autonomy by design, hypothetical Ryanair as AI-driven incumbent. Discuss implications cho strategy, organizational design, governance.

## Insight có thể áp dụng ngay
1. **AI as strategy, not tool**: Khi adopt AI, don't just augment existing processes. Rethink entire business model around AI capabilities. Ví dụ: traditional retailer → don't just add AI chatbot. Rethink: AI manages inventory, pricing, customer relationships autonomously.

2. **Synthetic competition preparation**: Competition sẽ shift to machine-level speed. Firms need AI-native competitive advantages. Ví dụ: khi competitors deploy AI agents negotiating prices real-time, manual pricing strategies become obsolete.

3. **Offload decision-making gradually**: ABMs gradually offload human decisions to AI. Start với low-stakes, expand. Ví dụ: start với AI managing routine inventory decisions, expand to pricing, then supplier negotiations.
